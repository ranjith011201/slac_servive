#  SURVIVE 

## Survive is a Mobile Application which connects the Donors with the needy people.


-Share videos, photos and details of people in need of help with their location.
-This app encourages people to help others in any difficult situations.
-Share your bio as a contributer who can help other people.
-NGO's can also join in as contributor, so that they get broader view locally and also globally.
-Share the posts via other social media also.
-This app can be used particularly during disasters like Floods, Earthquakes, etc.
-This app can also be used for medical emergencies like, need for Blood Donors, etc.
